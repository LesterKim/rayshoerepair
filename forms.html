Instructions


  1. Chrome users: Fill out the empty fields on the blank repair form. Then scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the printer icon to print the form out.

    Firefox users: Print out blank repair form and fill it out manually.

    Internet Explorer users: Fill out the empty fields on the blank repair form. Then click on the printer icon on the top right to print it out.

  2. Put your items in a box with a check or money order payable to "Ray's Shoe Repair" along with the printed repair form.
    (Note: we only accept check or money order for online transactions)

  3. Mail to the following address:

    Ray's Shoe Repair
    458 Hawkins Avenue
    Ronkonkoma, NY 11779

  4. Your repaired items will be returned in 7-10 business days. Thank you for your service.

The online repair form is currently unavailable for mobile viewing.
