About

Since 1969, we have provided shoe repair services using only the highest-quality materials. At Ray's Shoe Repair, we value nothing more than customer satisfaction. When our customers walk out of our store, we make sure that they leave happy with their repaired shoes.

Our shoe repairmen can also repair a wide array of leather goods, such as leather jackets, pocketbooks, belts, luggage, briefcases, UGG boots, boat covers, holsters, saddles, harnesses, hunting & hiking gear, and custom orthopedic orders. If you doubt we can repair a leather good, try us!
